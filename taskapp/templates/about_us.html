About us

We welcome you to join our community and discover the ease with which we can manage our little one’s time as well as ours.

Hey there! We're Tracy & George, the proud parents behind Tiny Tasker. We understand the joys and challenges of parenthood, and we're on a mission to make the lives of parents a bit easier. As parents to a 9-year-old girl and a 4-year-old boy, we've experienced the chaos of managing our children's extracurricular activities. That's how we created Tiny Tasker, an application designed to streamline, schedule, manage, discover and pay for our little one’s extracurricular activities.

Tiny Tasker

Tiny Tasker is not just an app; it's your partner in parenting.

Simplifying Parenting

Our primary goal is to help parents effortlessly discover, organize and monitor their children's extracurricular activities.

One Activity at a Time.

With features like activity scheduling, payment tracking, and a vibrant community, Tiny Tasker ensures that parents stay on top of their game.
